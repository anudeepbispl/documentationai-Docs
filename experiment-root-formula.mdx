# **🔥 ONE COMPLETE EXAMPLE (Fire)**

## **🧠 1. Raw Experience**

A small child:

- Sees a **bright flame**

- Feels **heat on the hand**

👉 No understanding yet — just sensation

---

## **🔍 2. Pattern Recognition**

After a few times:

- Every time near flame → feels heat

- Touching flame → pain

👉 Brain notices:

“Flame = hot = pain”

---

## **🧩 3. Assumption (basic rule forms)**

The brain creates a simple rule:

“Fire is dangerous”

This is not proven — just assumed from pattern.

---

## **📚 4. Knowledge (structured understanding)**

Later, with learning:

- “Fire produces heat due to combustion”

- “Heat transfers energy and can burn skin”

👉 Now it becomes **scientific knowledge**

---

# **🧪 Feynman-style full chain**

Child doesn’t start with physics.\
They feel heat → notice pattern → assume danger → later learn the science.

---

# **🎯 Final One-Line Flow**

**See fire → feel heat → notice repeat → assume danger → learn theory**

---

This is exactly how:

- Humans learn

- Science develops

- Even math ideas begin (at a higher abstraction level)
