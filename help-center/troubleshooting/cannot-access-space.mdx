---
title: Cannot Access My Space
description: Fix issues accessing your RaaziSpace spaces.
---

## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" error message
- Space does not appear in your dashboard

## Causes

- Incorrect permissions
- Logged in to wrong account

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your permissions">
    Ensure your account is added to the space with proper access rights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch accounts">
    Log out and back in with the correct RaaziSpace account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
