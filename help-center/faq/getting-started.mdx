## Overview

To start using RaaziSpace, create a new space for your documentation projects.

## Steps

1. Login to your RaaziSpace account.
2. Click on "New Space" on your dashboard.
3. Enter a name and description.
4. Click "Create" to finish.

## Tip

You can invite collaborators by using the "Members" tab in your new space.
