## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

  - An email address for account verification

    - Optional: Existing project documents in Markdown, PDF, or text for.   mat

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  RaaziSpace works best with stable internet connection. No software installation required—everything runs in your browser.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for RaaziSpace in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="globe" title-type="p">
    Navigate to [https://app.raazispace.com/signup](https://app.raazispace.com/signup).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.

    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from RaaziSpace. Click it to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in" title-type="p">
    Return to [https://app.raazispace.com/login](https://app.raazispace.com/login) and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="home" title-type="p">
    You'll land on the main dashboard. Click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Enter a name like "My Project Docs", add a description, and select visibility (private or public).

    Click **Create Space**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Space" icon="edit-3" title-type="p">
    In your new space, update the sidebar menu, add a custom logo, or set brand colors via the settings panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Content

Upload or create documents directly in your space. Choose your preferred method.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload File" icon="upload">
    Drag and drop files or click **Upload** to add PDFs, images, or Markdown files. RaaziSpace auto-indexes them for search.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Paste Markdown" icon="file-text">
    Create a new page with **New Page**, then paste your Markdown content.

    Here's a starter template:

    ```markdown
    # Welcome to My Project

    ## Overview

    This space organizes all project documentation.

    ## Quick Links

    - [Setup Guide](/setup)
    - [API Reference](/api)
    ```

    Preview and publish instantly.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Import from GitHub" icon="github">
    Connect your repo via **Integrations** > **GitHub**. Sync Markdown files automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Use Markdown for rich formatting. RaaziSpace supports tables, code blocks, and embeds out of the box.
</Callout>
